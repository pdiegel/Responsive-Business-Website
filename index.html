About Us

Ink & Ember: Pioneering Modern Brand Solutions

Welcome to Ink & Ember Creatives, where we specialize in elevating brands through comprehensive services in brand identity, social media management, CRM, and corporate travel management.

Operating remotely, we bring our expertise directly to entrepreneurs and businesses seeking innovative and effective solutions to stand out in competitive markets.

Our Expertise

Founded by the dynamic duo, Tabitha Franzwa and Vanessa Byas, our agency thrives on creativity and detailed organizational skills.

Tabitha, our Brand Identity Manager and CEO, specializes in turning your vision into a visual and emotional brand story that resonates across all platforms.

Vanessa, our COO, excels in creating seamless client experiences and sophisticated travel arrangements, ensuring every aspect of your business interactions is flawless.

Meet the Team

Amani (AJ) Vasquez — Social Media Manager & CM&FO

Amani, better known as AJ, is a dynamic force at Ink & Ember Creatives, seamlessly blending her financial acumen with a natural flair for social media.

Known for her charismatic presence and strategic acumen, AJ is pivotal in enhancing our social media engagements and financial operations.

Her passion for connecting people and her robust financial management skills make her an invaluable asset to our team.

Tabitha Franzwa — Founder, CEO & Brand Identity Manager

After 15 years in a career that didn't fulfill her, Tabitha turned her lifelong passion for art and graphic design into a thriving career by co-founding Ink & Ember Creatives.

With deep roots in a small town, Tabitha understands the drive to carve out one's own path and the resilience needed to succeed.

Her journey, marked by a strong influence from her hardworking mother and personal health challenges, fuels her commitment to creating meaningful and impactful brand identities.

Vanessa Byas — Co-Founder, COO & Corporate Travel Manager

Vanessa brings a wealth of experience in customer service and a lifelong passion for travel planning to her role at Ink & Ember Creatives.

With two degrees and nearly a decade at Papa John's, coupled with a brief tenure at Spectrum, Vanessa has honed her skills in customer relations and operations management. Her early experience planning vacations for her family sparked her love for travel, which she now combines with her extensive customer service expertise as our CRM and Corporate Travel Manager.

Driven by a vision for something greater, Vanessa co-founded Ink & Ember Creatives to redefine excellence in corporate travel and customer relations.
